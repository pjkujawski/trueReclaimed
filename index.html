Reclaimed Lumber

True Reclaimed Lumber Supply is your place for genuine 100+ year old lumber from barns all across the USA. We offer custom cut and profiled reclaimed barn wood, table tops, bar tops, barn doors, & paneling.

Lets work together

True Reclaimed was formed with the sole purpose of preserving history. We pride ourselves in bringing this history to Colorado. With the spirit of the west and rustic nature of the buildings, we believe we have a lot to offer.

Reclaimed Lumber Supply

Reclaimed lumber is our passion, this passion is the driving force for what we do at True Reclaimed. We travel all over the country hand picking old structures and materials to be brought back to Colorado. This gives us the opportunity to bring the best pieces back to our shop and turn them into something great.

Design Build Services

One of the greatest parts of working with True Reclaimed is the design build side of the business. From here we can take a concept and turn it into a reality that lasts. We have a 5000 square foot shop and several incredibly talented woodworkers in the shop that have been building furniture for decades. We use the highest quality standards in the industry and build furniture to last a life time.

Rocky Mountain Rustic Lumber

True Reclaimed offers an affordable solution for your rustic design without breaking the bank. We have developed a fantastic product for interior, exterior siding, board and baton, accent walls or any other design element that calls for that antiqued look. This is a Colorado product taken from fire mitigations, milled on the front range and finished right here in Wellington. We offer several different finishes and can create a custom look as well. Stock finishes include – Kodiak Brown, White Wash, Charcoal and Antiqued Grey.

Saw and Mill Services

Our mill allows us to cut a large variety of products from ranging from 34-inch-wide slabs, up too 20 foot long beams, live edge lumber or any special dimension that the job might call for. We also offer a mobile mill service, this is a great option for homeowners or contractors with trees on the property that they have plans for.

Contractor Supply

We are proud to work with many of Colorado’s fine builders. True Reclaimed provides rough or finished products for contractors giving them materials that are easy to use at a great price. We understand the building process and want to make buying and ordering these products a pleasure. Use us as a resource for commercial and residential projects.
