Reclaimed Lumber Supply

Reclaimed lumber is our passion, this passion is the driving force for what we do at True Reclaimed. We travel all over the country hand picking old structures and materials to be brought back to Colorado. This gives us the opportunity to bring the best pieces back to our shop and turn them into something great.

Rough Cut Lumber

Reclaiming lumber isn't just about re-purposing century year old barns. Trees that have been cut due to beetle infestations or to maintain healthy forests can be milled into usable lumber as well, which is a great alternative to buying harvested timbers for your next project. Rather your building custom furniture or putting up a snow fence we can cut the lumber you'll need.

Mobile Sawmill Services

Our mill allows us to cut a large variety of products from ranging from 34-inch-wide slabs, up too 20 foot long beams, live edge lumber or any special dimension that the job might call for. We also offer a mobile mill service, this is a great option for homeowners or contractors with trees on the property that they have plans for.
